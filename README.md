I'm Thiago. For years I shipped frontend; now I'm focused on a sharper edge:
**AI Product Engineer** — building products where AI is the core, and owning the
experience layer most people get wrong.

## What I'm building toward

Mastering the full stack of AI-native development:

- **Context engineering** — designing the context that makes models reliable
- **Building products with the API** — tool use, structured outputs, streaming
- **Agents & MCP** — giving models hands, not just a mouth
- **Reliability** — evals and observability for AI systems
- **AI UX** — the experience layer (my home turf): streaming, generative UI,
  failing gracefully when the model surprises you
